About


KOPIMung. Cafe

En. Syafiq Ikhwan establish this café in Kuala Terengganu's Kampung Pancur Kuin. The café employs a total of five people. We provide a side menu of maggi cooked over a wood burner in addition to the espresso and frappe menu. In addition to informal dishes like maggi, we provide munchies including muffins, lasagna, and buns.
